SuperBrain Extension

Connect to your SuperBrain server

Server Configuration

The URL where your SuperBrain backend is running.
Example: http://192.168.1.100:5000 or https://your-server.com

Find this token in your SuperBrain backend terminal when you start the server, or check the server settings.

Features

Bulk Import Saved Posts

Import all your Instagram saved posts with a single click. The extension scans and processes each post.

Sync Collections

Your Instagram collections are recreated in SuperBrain with the same names and icons. Posts are automatically placed in the correct collections.

Background Processing

Posts are sent to your backend for AI analysis. Videos get transcribed, images get tagged, and everything becomes searchable.

How to Use

1

Configure Settings

Enter your SuperBrain server URL and access token above, then click "Save Settings".

2

Open Instagram Saved

Navigate to your Instagram profile and go to the "Saved" section, or visit any collection.

3

Click Import

Click the SuperBrain extension icon in your browser toolbar, then click "Scan Instagram Saved".

4

Watch the Magic

The extension will scroll through your posts, extract URLs, and send them to SuperBrain for analysis.
